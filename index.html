Who we're

AMPZ is the LinkedIn for sports in Africa, connecting talents to a world of opportunities. Our mobile app facilitates talent to opportunity matching by helping talents curate their data and content while giving them access to verified opportunities and scouts.

Our Vision:

We envision “an Africa where every dream is fulfilled in the world of sports”

Our Mision

Our mission is to "harness the power of sports and technology to impact lives in Africa”

Our Solution

AMPZ is leveraging on technology as a tool to tackle issues around access to information and exploitation in African sports.

Access: Giving talents access to verified information on opportunities and providing access for scouts/agents to talent data and content.

Platform: Providing a platform for talents to curate their sports profile, showcase themselves and connect to the right audience.

Exploitation: Building a safe community protecting talents from the risk of exploitation and trafficking in their career journey.
